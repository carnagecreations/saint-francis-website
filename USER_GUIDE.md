## How Forms Work (The Important Part)

### Visitor Submits Form
When someone fills out any form on your website (contact, volunteer, adoption, foster), they submit it by clicking the button. Here's what happens:

1. **Their info goes to your admin dashboard** (SanctuaryBase)
2. **You see it in the Inbox** within 1 minute
3. **You can reply via email** from the dashboard

### Available Forms
| Form | Where | What you receive |
|------|-------|------------------|
| Contact Form | Contact page | Name, email, message |
| Adoption Application | Adopt page | Full adoption application |
| Foster Application | Foster page | Full foster application |
| Volunteer Signup | Volunteer page | Volunteer info & availability |
| Questions/General | Contact page | Any general inquiry |

### What You Do With Them
1. Open **SanctuaryBase** (your admin dashboard)
2. Go to **Inbox** tab
3. Read the submission
4. Click **"Send reply"**
5. Type your response
6. Click **"Send reply"**
7. Applicant receives email within 1-2 minutes

## How Donations Work

### Online Donations
- Visitor goes to Donate page
- They choose amount (slider from $10–$500)
- Click "Donate $X now"
- Opens **Zeffy** (payment processor)
- They pay securely
- **You receive:** Email notification about donation amount
- **Tax receipt:** Automatically sent to donor

### In-Kind Donations
- Visitor clicks "Inquire about donating" on In-Kind Donations
- This opens contact form with subject pre-filled
- They describe what they want to donate
- Submission comes to your Inbox
- You reply with instructions

### Car Donations
- Visitor clicks "Donate your car" 
- Contact form opens with subject pre-filled
- They provide vehicle details
- You handle logistics and tax documentation

## Common Scenarios

### Scenario 1: Someone wants to adopt
1. They fill Adoption Application on website
2. Appears in your Inbox within 1 minute
3. You can review their answers
4. Reply with next steps (screening questions, home visit, etc.)

**What you do:**
- Read their answers carefully
- Ask any follow-up questions in your reply
- Check references if listed
- Decide if animal is good fit
- Arrange next steps in follow-up emails

### Scenario 2: Someone wants to donate a horse trailer
1. They click "In-Kind Donations"
2. Fill form with item details
3. Submission appears in Inbox
4. You reply with: "Yes! We can use this. Here's where to drop it off..."

**What you do:**
- Assess if item is useful
- Arrange pickup/drop-off
- Thank them in reply

### Scenario 3: Someone donated $50
1. You receive Zeffy notification (email)
2. Donor gets automatic tax receipt
3. Donation logged in SanctuaryBase Finance tab (admin team handles)

**What you do:**
- No action needed—automatic
- Finance team tracks it

### Scenario 4: Someone wants to volunteer
1. They click "Volunteer" and fill form
2. Appears in Inbox under "Volunteer" category
3. You review and reply with orientation date/time

**What you do:**
- Review availability and skills
- Schedule orientation
- Reply with details

## Troubleshooting: "Something Seems Wrong"

### Problem: Form submitted, but I don't see it in Inbox
**Try this:**
1. Hard refresh your browser (Ctrl+Shift+R)
2. Log out and log back in
3. Wait 2 minutes and refresh
4. If still missing, check your spam folder for confirmation email
5. Contact: admin@yumareptiles.com

### Problem: Visitor says they didn't get my reply email
**Try this:**
1. Check Inbox — did it show "✓ Replied"?
2. Ask visitor to check spam/junk folder
3. Ask them to confirm email address was correct on form
4. Try replying again with phone number in message

### Problem: Donate button not working
1. Try in different browser
2. Try on phone (mobile)
3. If still broken, refresh page (Ctrl+F5)
4. Try donating a small amount ($10) to test
5. Contact: admin@yumareptiles.com

### Problem: Website looks weird/broken
1. Hard refresh (Ctrl+Shift+R)
2. Clear browser cache and cookies
3. Try in different browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari)
4. Try on mobile
5. Contact: admin@yumareptiles.com

## When to Contact Your Tech Person

You need tech help if:
- ❌ Website down or not loading
- ❌ Forms not submitting
- ❌ Inbox not showing submissions
- ❌ Can't send email replies
- ❌ Donations not processing
- ❌ Images not loading on Animals page

**Don't need tech help if:**
- ✅ You want to reply to a message (just use Inbox)
- ✅ You want to update donation amounts (ask admin team)
- ✅ You want to add new animals (admin team handles via Firestore)
