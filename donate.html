Questions about your gift?

Where does my donation go?
100% of donations go directly to animal care: food, veterinary treatment, shelter maintenance, bedding, medications, emergency surgery, and enrichment. We have zero paid staff—every team member is a volunteer. There's no overhead eating into your gift.
Is my donation tax-deductible?
Yes. Saint Francis Rescue and Sanctuary is a registered 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization (EIN: 99-0599742). All donations are fully tax-deductible to the extent allowed by U.S. law. Request a donation receipt by emailing contact@saintfrancisrescue.org with proof of your donation.
Can I make a recurring monthly donation?
Yes. Zeffy supports monthly recurring donations, and you can set it up to automatically give the same amount each month. Monthly donors are our lifeline—they let us plan feed purchases and routine vet care with confidence.
Do you accept major gifts or bequests?
Absolutely. If you're interested in a substantial donation, stock donation, planned giving, or memorial gift, please contact us directly. We'd love to discuss how we can honor your legacy.
Can I donate supplies instead of money?
Yes. We're always grateful for hay, grain, medications, veterinary services, and other supplies. Email contact@saintfrancisrescue.org to coordinate donations.
Will I get updates on how my donation was used?
For major gifts, absolutely. We'll send you a personalized impact statement. You can also follow us on Instagram and Facebook to see real-time updates on the animals you're helping save.
